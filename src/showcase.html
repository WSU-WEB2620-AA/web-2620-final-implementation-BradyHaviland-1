Ice Cubes And Fireballs Spotlight

Concept

In this group team game, you want to collect the elemental tokens that correspond to your team. Every round two players will be summoned to duel, you will have to figure out who is on your team, deception is encouraged. Capture the elemental tokens for your team to win.

Rules

Setup

Determine how many players are playing, you will need an even number. This game can accommodate 10-50 players.

Once the amount of players is known count out a set of cards, half will be Ice Cubes and the other half will be Fire Balls. Example: If 10 players are playing count out 5 Ice Cube cards and 5 Fire Ball cards.

Shuffle the Ice Cube cards and Fire Ball cards deal one out to every player.

Next, grab the number cards. Since in this scenario 10 people are playing grab the number cards indicating 1-10. Shuffle those and deal one card to each player.

Put a table in the middle with two bowls, one bowl with the elemental Ice Cube Tokens and one bowl with the Fire Ball tokens. Put the two stuffed throwables in the middle as well. Make a circle of chairs or seats around the center table. Have every player pick a seat, and have every player remember their team color and their number.

Gameplay

Pick any player and that player will call out two numbers. (You can't call out your own number). Those players whose numbers were called stand up and will potentially duel. The goal is to grab an elemental token off the table. Yet the other player will try to stop you by chucking the throwable that corresponds to their team to hit you. Once you have been hit you can't grab a token.

Potentially two players from the same team could be pitted against each other, friendly fire is active, you can take out your teammates. This is when the deception comes in. You can lie and say what team you're on. Just you can only pick up the throwable and elemental token that belong to you.

As long as you grab an elemental token and return to an empty seat you are safe regardless if you make an attempt to throw the throwable at someone or not.

Players can get up as fast or as quickly as they want from the chair when their numbers are called.

The player who sits down last will call the next two numbers, and the cycle continues.

Winning the Game

Your team wins the game once your team has collected all your elemental tokens.
