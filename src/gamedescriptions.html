Here are four prototypes in the works.

Challenge Accepted

Is a multi-tasking speed card game. Players each have the same deck in their color, their goal is to get rid of all of their cards before their opponents. Yet according to the objective played and the amount determined for how many players can complete the task adds strategy and high-stakes.

*Images coming soon*

Maze Mages

This board game has moving platform tiles that can be rotated and moved. This is a capture the potion style game where all the players are deemed mages and every color has special abilities. Inscrease your stats, defend your region, trap players, and steal their potion and return to your base. Be the last mage left to win.

*Images coming soon*

Dye

In this strategy board game everyone has a target that they must eliminate, targets are decided randomly. You can only go after your target, even though the person pursuing may not be your target at all. All players have a deadly poisonous color they cannot touch. Make temporary alliances, and note other players deadly colors as it will be important later.

*Images coming soon*

Ice Cubes and Fireballs

In this group team game, you want to collect the elemental tokens that correspond to your team. Every round two players will be summoned to duel, you will have to figure out who is on your team, deception is encouraged. Capture the elemental tokens for your team to win.
